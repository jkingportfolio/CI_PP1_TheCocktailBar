About us

Welcome to The Cocktail Bars official website. Opening in 2019 The Cocktail Bar has grown to be one of if not the best Cocktail Bar in the world. From our small beginnings to the globalally recognised bar we are now we have continued to deliver the most classy, tasty and affordable drinks in the bar scene. Now not only James Bond can have a world class Vodka Martini shaken not stirred but you can too! We look forward to welcoming you into The Cocktail Bar where you will have an experience you will never forget.
